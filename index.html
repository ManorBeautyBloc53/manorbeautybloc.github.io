About Manor Beauty Bloc

Manor Beauty Bloc was created to fill a real need in our community. We offer a place where affordable, quality beauty products, expert services, and genuine care come together under one roof. Founded by a local entrepreneur who took a leap of faith from corporate leadership into business ownership, MBB is built on passion, purpose, and service.

We’re proud to be Manor’s go-to beauty supply and salon, offering premium braiding hair, styling products, and professional services designed for real people and real lifestyles. What makes us different? We don’t just sell products, we help you feel confident, seen, and supported every time you walk through our doors.
